About SATI

Established in 1960 in Vidisha, MP. Founded by Late Maharaja Jiwajirao Scindia and inaugurated by Dr. Rajendra Prasad. NAAC certified and NBA accredited with 60+ years of technical education excellence.

Our Mission

Providing SATI community with an intelligent, accessible digital assistant that enhances learning experiences and simplifies access to institutional resources.

Our Vision

Revolutionizing student interaction with educational resources through cutting-edge AI technology, making information discovery seamless and efficient.
