Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access study materials?
Navigate to the Resources page from the main menu. You can browse by category (Notes, Practical Assignments, Test Papers, etc.) or use the search function to find specific materials for your branch and subject.
Is SATI ChatBot free to use?
Yes! SATI ChatBot is completely free for all SATI students, faculty, and staff. Our mission is to make educational resources accessible to everyone in the SATI community.
How accurate is the AI chatbot?
Our AI chatbot is trained on comprehensive SATI-specific data and uses advanced language models. While we strive for high accuracy, we recommend verifying important information with official sources or faculty members.
Can I request new features or resources?
Absolutely! We welcome feature requests and resource suggestions. Use the contact form above with "Feature Request" or "Resource Request" as the subject, and we'll consider it for future updates.
How do I report a bug or technical issue?
Please use the contact form with "Bug Report" as the subject. Include details about the issue, your browser/device information, and steps to reproduce the problem. We'll investigate and fix it as soon as possible.
Is my data secure when using the chatbot?
Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Conversations are not stored permanently, and we don't collect personal information unless explicitly provided. All communications are encrypted and handled securely.
